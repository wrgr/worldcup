Sources & methodology

Predictions (ELO): each unresolved match uses the embedded ELO table and the logistic transform 1/(1+10^(-ΔELO/(400×upset))), where upset is the Upset-factor slider. It otherwise ignores injuries, rest, travel, home advantage, and styles except as reflected in the rating. Source: Economics Observatory 2026 knockout preview.

Betting market & cards & news: the Recent-data layer pulls DraftKings moneylines (converted to a “to advance” probability), yellow/red cards, and match/league news headlines from ESPN's public summary endpoint. With the layer on, the shown probability is (1−w)·ELO + w·market.

Live scoreboard: the page first reads scoreboard.json from the repo, then ESPN's public World Cup scoreboard feed, then the embedded snapshot. Official fixtures/results should be checked against FIFA's page.
